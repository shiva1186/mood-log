Mood Log

by Eric Burke

Mood Log tracks how you feel throughout the day. You can type new words or select from a list of words you've already logged. Each log entry contains the word, how strongly you feel, and the current date and time.

Words are limited to 30 characters -- Mood Log is not intended for complex sentences or journal entries.
